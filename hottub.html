HotTub

HotTub is a mobile application that re-imagines the group chat experience. How do we do this? We have removed everyone's names within the chat so you cannot identify who is who. HotTub allows users to cast a vote to expose their friends and reveal who that message is from.

HOTTUB IS AN ON-GOING BUILD IN ACTIVE DEVELOPMENT WITH AN EXPECTED RELEASE OF SUMMER 2019!

UX Problems

Throughout designing and developing HotTub, we encountered many UX issues we needed to work out. One of the most notable and challenging of these issues was the reaction bubbles. We wanted to allow our users to be able to react to messages easily and quickly. We noticed other messaging apps like iMessage and Messanger have these features, however, they are not easily accessible by the user therefore nobody uses them.

We wanted our reaction system to be a first class feature, easily accessible and not hidden behind a gesture. When designing this, I quickly realized this was more challenging then I initially thought. Placing a button on the screen was the best way to highlight and remind users of this feature, but adding a button compromised readability of the messages.

This required some explorations and testing to find what the best way to accomplish this goal was. Users complained that they did not like the iMessage reaction system because it was a hard to perform gesture (long press) and it was too slow to display. In order for our system to be better, we needed our menu to be a quick and easily accessible.

After some user testing we found that the best solution was to have a small arrow inside the messages bubble that the users can either slide or tap on to access the reactions draw. This will also display the time on the right side of the message. We found that this was least distracting when using the app but also reminded user that they can quickly react to a friends message. Based on our initial testing we anticipate a huge increase in the amount of reactions being sent when compared to other apps like iMessage.

HotTub's Pages

After some designing I decided to go with a flowing water theme for our app that can be distinguished across all our our platforms. After deciding on this theme it became very easy to wire frame out other portions of our site and implement features as necessary.

Below you can see the different pages across our site. A similar evolution of each of HotTub's pages took place beginning with initial sketch to wire frame through the final design. Each page is continuously being tweaked as the development of HotTub takes place.

It was very important in the design phase to ensure our site was responsive. We wanted to ensure our website will look great and work well on a desktop (or laptop), a tablet, and a mobile phone’s browser.

Responsive web design creates a system for a single site to react to the size of a user’s device—with one URL and one content source. Our sites design offers a fluid and flexible layout which adjusts according to screen size.
