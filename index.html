About Me

My name is Nick Carbone. I am a passionate user experience and product designer. I construct meaningful and efficient solutions to user's problems by applying scenario-based design and utilizing the user-centered design process. At the moment, I am based in New York City. If you would like to get in contact with me, feel free to email me at njcbone@gmail.com.

When I'm not designing or building apps, you will find me with my dog, Charlie, outside on hikes or long walks up the Hudson waterfront trail. I also really enjoy exploring new parts of the city and riding trails while crusing around on my electric skateboard.
